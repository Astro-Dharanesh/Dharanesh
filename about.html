The Journey into Astrophysics

My interest in astronomy began with a simple curiosity about the night sky, which quickly evolved into a rigorous pursuit of physics. My journey focuses heavily on observational astronomy, utilizing skills gained from the International Asteroid Search Campaign (IASC) and various workshops on data analysis. I aim to contribute to the field of exoplanetary science, using computational methods to process large datasets and uncover new celestial objects.

My Philosophy

My belief is that the universe is the ultimate laboratory, and through dedication to data analysis and observation, we can uncover its deepest secrets. I am committed to open science, collaborative research, and effective public outreach to share the wonder of astrophysics with the next generation.
