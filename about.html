The Journey into Astrophysics

My interest in astronomy began with a simple curiosity about the night sky, which quickly evolved into a rigorous pursuit of physics. “Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known.” These words reflect something deeply personal, my constant urge to ask why, and my growing admiration for the universe’s mysteries Dr. Abdul Kalam's contribution to India's development in space technology and his journey from a humble background to becoming a great scientist and leader of our country has profoundly inspired me. My current interests are Exoplanets, Heliophysics and Planetary science. My journey so far has been shaped by curiosity, hands-on learning. I believe my journey in Physics will not only deepen my knowledge but also make me a researcher and help me to contribute to our scientific community. I’m excited to move towards my dream of working in space science.

My Philosophy

My belief is that the universe is the ultimate laboratory, and through dedication to data analysis and observation, we can uncover its deepest secrets. I am committed to open science, collaborative research, and effective public outreach to share the wonder of astrophysics with the next generation.
