The Journey into Astrophysics

My interest in astronomy began with a simple curiosity about the night sky, which quickly evolved into a rigorous pursuit of physics. “Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known.” These words reflect something deeply personal: my constant urge to ask why and my growing admiration for the universe’s mysteries.

Dr. Abdul Kalam's contribution to India's development in space technology and his journey from a humble background to becoming a great scientist and leader has profoundly inspired me. My current interests lie in Exoplanets, Heliophysics, and Planetary Science. My journey so far has been shaped by curiosity and hands-on learning. I believe my path in Physics will not only deepen my knowledge but also help me contribute meaningfully to the scientific community as a researcher. I’m excited to move towards my dream of working in space science.

My Philosophy

I believe that the universe is the ultimate laboratory. Through dedication to data analysis and observation, we can uncover its deepest secrets. I am committed to open science, collaborative research, and effective public outreach to share the wonder of astrophysics with the next generation.
