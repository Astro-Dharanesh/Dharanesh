Vesta: Goddess of the Hearth

January 20, 2026

Vesta, the second largest object in the asteroid belt, is one of the most scientifically important asteroids providing insights into our solar system...

It is located in the main belt at a distance of 2.36 AU from the Sun and has a rotation period of 5.34 hours...

The Dawn mission confirmed Vesta as the parent body of HED meteorites and revealed extensive geological activity...

Organic-rich material discovered near the Cranach crater suggests delivery by carbonaceous impacts...
