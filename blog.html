Why This Blog Exists

Purpose & Scope

This blog is created as a personal academic journal to document my journey in physics and astronomy. It serves as a space where I share my learning, research experiences, and observations while working on some astronomical projects and scientific literature. My primary motivation for creating this blog is to maintain a transparent and structured record of my academic growth.

The articles published here fall broadly into three categories:

  • Research Notes: Technical write-ups based on data analysis, observational studies, and ongoing research projects.
  • Conceptual Articles: Explanations of astronomical and physical concepts aimed at students and enthusiasts.
  • Outreach & Reflection: Summaries of seminars, workshops, conferences, and public science activities that I have participated.

In the long term, this blog also functions as a research portfolio. It reflects my academic interests in Astrophysics.

Readers are encouraged to explore the articles listed alongside this page, which represent both completed studies and evolving research directions.
